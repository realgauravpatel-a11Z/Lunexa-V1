1. Data We Collect

Lunexa is a frontend-only demo application. All data you enter — including your name, email, address, and order details — is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. No data is transmitted to any server.

  • Account information (name, email, password)
  • Shipping address
  • Order history
  • Wishlist and cart contents

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the shopping experience within your browser session:

  • To display your profile, cart, wishlist and order history
  • To pre-fill checkout forms for convenience
  • To simulate order tracking and status updates

We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third party.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored in your browser's localStorage under the key prefix lunexa_. Clearing your browser data or using private/incognito mode will remove all stored information. You can clear your data at any time from your browser settings.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

This application uses localStorage (not cookies) to persist your session and preferences. A cookie consent banner is shown on first visit. Your choice is stored in localStorage under lunexa_cookie_consent.

5. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally in your browser, you have full control:

  • View your data in your browser's developer tools under Application → Local Storage
  • Delete all data by clearing your browser storage
  • Use incognito mode for a session that leaves no trace

6. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at our contact page or email privacy@lunexa.in.
