The Founder's Vision

Hi, I'm Patel Gaurav — founder of Lunexa. Growing up in Bengaluru, I was always fascinated by technology, but frustrated by the gap between what the world offered and what was truly accessible in India.

I started Lunexa in 2023 after spending years in product design and noticing that premium tech products were either overpriced, hard to find, or came with zero post-purchase support. That had to change.

Lunexa is my answer to that problem. A carefully curated store, every single product hand-tested and genuinely warranted, delivered with the care you'd expect from a friend who really knows tech.
