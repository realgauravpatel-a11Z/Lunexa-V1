1. Data We Collect

Lunexa is a frontend-only application. All data you enter — name, email, address, orders — is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. No data is transmitted to external servers except optionally to our Google Sheets integration (if configured).

  • Account: name, email, encrypted password
  • Delivery address
  • Order history and cart contents
  • Wishlist items and cookie preferences

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Lunexa shopping experience:

  • Display your profile, cart, wishlist and order history
  • Pre-fill checkout forms for convenience
  • Simulate order tracking and status updates

We do not sell, share or transmit your data for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored in localStorage under the prefix lunexa_. Clearing browser data removes all stored information. Incognito mode leaves no trace.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

This app uses localStorage (not HTTP cookies) to persist sessions and preferences. Your consent choice is stored locally. Manage preferences at Cookie Settings.

5. Your Rights

  • View your data in browser devtools (Application → Local Storage)
  • Delete all data by clearing browser storage
  • Use incognito mode for session-only usage
