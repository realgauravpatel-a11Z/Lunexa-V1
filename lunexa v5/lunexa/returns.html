1. Return Window

We offer a 30-day return window from the date of delivery. Items must be returned within 30 days of receiving your order.

2. Eligible Items

To be eligible, items must be in original unused condition with all original packaging, accessories, and documentation. Non-eligible: opened software, digital downloads, consumables, and misused products.

3. Return Process

  • Contact devleadlabs@gmail.com within 30 days of delivery
  • Include your order number and reason for return
  • We will provide return authorization within 24 hours
  • Pack securely and ship using a trackable method

4. Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method. Return shipping is borne by the customer unless the item is defective.

5. Exchanges

Defective or damaged items will be replaced at no cost. Contact devleadlabs@gmail.com with photos of the damage and your order number.
